Sparse autoencoders on vision transformers

Using sparse-autoencoder interpretability to ask what visual cues individual ViT features actually learn.

// image placeholder — SAE feature visualizations

Motivation

Sparse autoencoders have become a practical tool for finding interpretable, monosemantic features inside language models. The natural question: do they work on vision transformers, and what visual concepts do they surface?

What I'm looking at

  • Training sparse autoencoders on ViT activations across layers.
  • Characterizing learned features via dataset examples and synthetic maximization.
  • Comparing what different layers encode — from low-level texture to object-level cues.
