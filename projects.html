Client work

// satellite · computer vision

Vessel detection in satellite imagery

End-to-end detection pipeline for identifying and localizing vessels across large satellite scenes. Built around a data-centric active-learning loop to make the most of a limited labeling budget, with uncertainty-aware scoring to surface ambiguous detections for human review rather than silently shipping them.

object detection remote sensing active learning uncertainty
// satellite · analysis

Biochemical analysis from satellite imagery

Models that infer biochemical indicators from satellite data, with careful attention to distribution shift and confidence calibration. Emphasis on knowing when a prediction is reliable enough to act on — and when it isn't.

regression calibration distribution shift

Limitations & explainability of vision-language models

An ongoing study of where VLMs fail and why — probing failure modes through targeted evaluation and explainability techniques. The goal is a clearer, honest picture of what these models can and cannot be trusted to perceive.
